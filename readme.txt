This is an app what allows you to post your blog to many blog pages at one time.

To get the app working well , some python packages should be needed .
here is the list :
	beautifulsoup4-4.1.3
	poster-0.8.1
	python-2.7.3
	wxPython2.8-win32-unicode-2.8.12.1-py27
Cause of the app was created at the win32 platform , and not be tested on Linux
or Mac , so I'm not quite sure about it will gonna work well at the other platform .

The app was created just for fun at first , and later I found it will be very helpful
in the SEO work . Now I have made it work pretty well with the ‘ 51游戏社区 ’ (a blog
service provider in China) . If you know how to extend it , just take it and make it 
work . So enjoy your time .
